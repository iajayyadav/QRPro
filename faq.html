Frequently Asked Questions

What is a QR code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a two-dimensional barcode that stores information like URLs, contact data, and Wi-Fi credentials, which can be scanned using smartphones or QR scanners.

Is this QR code generator free?

Yes! Our QR code generator is completely free to use without any sign-up or subscription requirements.

Can I add my own logo to the QR code?

Absolutely! You can upload a logo or image to embed in the center of your QR code. Just make sure the logo file is under 500KB for best results.

Will my data be stored?

No. All QR code generation happens locally in your browser. We don’t collect or store any personal data or inputs.

What formats can I download the QR code in?

You can download the QR code in PNG, SVG, or PDF formats depending on your needs — for print or digital use.

Are the QR codes permanent?

Yes. Once generated, your QR code will work as long as the content (URL, contact info, etc.) remains valid. They do not expire.

Can I generate QR codes for Wi-Fi or vCards?

Yes! Our tool supports creating QR codes for URLs, plain text, phone numbers, email addresses, Wi-Fi credentials, and vCards (contact sharing).
